IDC Velocity Sales

Velocity Sales is a sales planning and operations tool that enables data-driven market targeting and account prioritization. Through sophisticated market segmentation and opportunity scoring, we help sales teams focus their efforts on high-value accounts with the greatest probability of conversion.

Core Platform Objectives

Optimize Market Definition and Discovery

  • Enable precise market definition across industry, geography, and technology parameters
  • Identify untapped market opportunities through cross-market analysis
  • Reduce time spent on market research and segmentation by 70%
  • Increase accuracy of market size estimation by 50%
  • Discover minimum 3 adjacent market opportunities per analysis

Enhance Account Targeting Precision

  • Generate comprehensive account lists based on market parameters
  • Calculate potential spend estimates for each account
  • Achieve 90% accuracy in spend forecasting for targeted accounts

Maximize Sales Team Efficiency

  • Reduce time spent on prospect identification by 60%
  • Increase successful engagement rate by 40% through opportunity scoring
  • Improve win rates by 25% through focused targeting
