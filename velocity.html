IDC Velocity Sales

Velocity> transforms sales planning and operations by providing data-driven market intelligence and account prioritization. By leveraging comprehensive market data, Velocity enables sales teams to precisely calculate their Total Addressable Market (TAM) and Service Addressable Market (SAM), identify ideal customer profiles (ICP), and focus their efforts on high-probability opportunities.

Core Platform Objectives

Velocity fundamentally transforms how organizations approach sales planning and market analysis. By combining comprehensive market intelligence with sophisticated analysis tools, teams are enabled to make data-driven decisions about market entry, resource allocation, and account prioritization. The platform's objectives center on bringing clarity to market sizing, identifying valuable opportunities, and maximizing sales team effectiveness through intelligent targeting.
